Our Story

At Charity: Water, our mission is simple yet profound: Bring clean and safe water to every single person on the planet.

We are dedicated to bringing clean water to communities in need. Our mission is to provide total transparency and real proof of your impact.

Imagine a world where no one has to walk miles for dirty water, where preventable diseases from unsafe waters are a thing of the past, and where communities can thrive because they have access to life-giving hydration. This is the future we are building here at Charity: Water.

How do we do it? We fund sustainable water solutions in developing countries, as we work hand-in-hand with trusted local partners on the ground. These partners are experts in their regions, ensuring that every project is tailored to the community's unique needs and built to last.

Every dollar you give goes straight to the field, making a tangible impact on real lives. We cover our operating costs through separate groups of private donors, so you can be confident that your generosity is bringing clean water to those who need it most.

Nia's Story
Nia's village suffered from dirty water, causing sickness and missed school. Charity: Water drilled a new well, bringing clean, safe water. Now, Nia and her community are healthier, attend school, and have a bright future all thanks to clean water.
